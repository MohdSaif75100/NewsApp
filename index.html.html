Here is a select list of stories to start the day HC hearing today on Kejriwal’s plea against his arrest

The court had allowed his wife Sunita Keriwal and his lawyers to meet him for 30 minutes everyday during the custody. The court also allowed them to carry his prescribed medicines and home-cooked food during the remand period. He was produced before Special Judge Amitabh Rawat. During the proceedings, the CBI- represented by DP Singh- sought court's permission to interrogate Kejriwal in the adjoining room so that CBI can proceed with his formal arrest. The CBI on Tuesday examined Kejriwal in Tihar Jail and recorded his statement related to the Excise Policy case.
